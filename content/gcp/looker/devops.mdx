

# Technical Interview Questions for DevOps Engineer - Looker Division

As a hiring manager for the Looker division at Google Cloud Platform, you are looking for a qualified devops engineer who can effectively manage deployment using Looker. In order to assess the technical skills and knowledge of the candidate, here is a list of technical questions and discussion topics that you can cover during the interview process.

## Looker Technical Knowledge

1. What is Looker and how does it work?
2. Can you explain the components of Looker's architecture?
3. What is the difference between PDTs and CDTs in Looker?
4. How do you optimize Looker performance?
5. What are LookML models and how do they relate to Looker?
6. How does Looker handle data modeling and transformations?
7. Can you explain the concept of Looker Explores and their purpose?
8. How does Looker handle data security and permissions?
9. How do you troubleshoot issues with Looker?
10. What are some common challenges in working with Looker and how do you address them?

## DevOps and Deployment Knowledge

1. What is your experience with managing deployments using Looker?
2. Can you walk me through the process of setting up a deployment pipeline for Looker?
3. How do you ensure high availability and scalability of Looker deployments?
4. What are some best practices for deploying Looker on different environments (e.g. development, staging, production)?
5. How do you handle version control for Looker releases?
6. Can you explain the concept of continuous integration and continuous deployment (CI/CD) and how it applies to Looker?
7. What tools and technologies are you familiar with for automation and orchestration of Looker deployments?
8. How do you monitor and analyze the performance of Looker deployments?
9. Can you share any experience with troubleshooting and resolving deployment issues in Looker?
10. What are some security considerations when managing Looker deployments?

## General Technical Skills

1. Can you tell us about your experience with programming languages and scripting (e.g. Python, SQL)?
2. How familiar are you with cloud technologies, specifically Google Cloud Platform?
3. Have you worked with any other business intelligence or analytics platforms? If yes, which ones and what was your role?
4. Are you comfortable working with data and data modeling?
5. How do you stay updated with new technologies and tools in the devops space?
6. Can you provide an example of a time when you had to troubleshoot and resolve a technical issue in a time-sensitive situation?
7. How do you approach collaboration and communication with cross-functional teams?
8. Do you have any experience with automation and configuration management tools (e.g Ansible, Puppet)?
9. Can you explain what infrastructure as code is and how it is beneficial in managing deployments?
10. Are you familiar with agile methodologies and how do you see it fitting into the devops process?

## Scenario-based Questions

1. Imagine that there is a performance issue with your Looker deployment, how would you troubleshoot it? 
2. How would you handle a situation where there is a data security breach in Looker?
3. What would you do if there was a sudden increase in traffic to your Looker instance?
4. How would you ensure that your Looker deployments are compliant with data regulations such as GDPR or CCPA?
5. Can you provide an example of a complex deployment you have managed using Looker?
